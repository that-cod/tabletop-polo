🎮 PROJECT OVERVIEW
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A physics-based, browser-playable tabletop polo game where you:
  • Click to move player tokens strategically
  • Drag from the mallet tip to flick the ball
  • Play 4 chukkas (7-min periods) against AI
  • Score goals by getting the ball into the opponent's goal zone

Tech Stack: Matter.js (physics) + Canvas (rendering) + Vite (build) + Vanilla JS
